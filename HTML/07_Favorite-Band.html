FLORENCE + THE MACHINE

Florence and the Machine (stylised as Florence + the Machine) are an English indie rock band formed in London in 2007. The band's music features dramatic, eccentric production and Welch's powerful vocals. Their sound has been described as a combination of various genres, including rock and soul.

Band Members

  • Florence Welch: Front-Woman, lead vocals, percussion, occasional piano
  • Isabella Summers: keyboards, backing vocals
  • Robert Ackroyd: lead guitar, backing vocals, bass
  • Tom Monger: harp, percussion, backing vocals
  • Cyrus Bayandor: bass
  • Aku Orraca-Tetteh: percussion, backing vocals, keyboards
  • Dionne Douglas: violin, backing vocals, rhythm guitar
  • Loren Humphrey: drums
